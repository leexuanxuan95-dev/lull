Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-08 · Last updated: 2026-05-08

Lull is built on a simple promise: your evening is your evening. We've designed the app so that almost nothing leaves your device. This policy explains what data is involved, where it lives, and the choices you have.

What we collect

The app stores the following locally on your device only:

  • Your first name, city, and the calm activity you entered during onboarding.
  • Your generated stories and chat messages with the Companion.
  • Your preferences (selected voice, sleep-timer default, smart-wake time).
  • Your subscription state, validated by Apple's StoreKit.

None of the items above are sent to Lull's servers. We do not operate a server that receives them.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect your sleep data, even when HealthKit is granted.
  • We do not collect the text of your stories or chat messages.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or analytics events.
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that collect personal information.

Story generation

Stories are generated entirely on-device by a local templated grammar engine. There is no LLM call, no cloud generation, and no network access required to read you a bedtime story. Lull works in airplane mode.

Companion chat

The Companion's replies are produced by a local rule-based engine. Your messages and the engine's replies stay on your device. Clearing the conversation in Settings → Companion → Clear conversation deletes the local store.

Voice clone (Pro+)

If you opt into the Pro+ voice clone feature, a 30-second voice sample is recorded with your microphone. The audio is processed on your device and the resulting voice profile is stored in your private iCloud Keychain — accessible only to you. Lull does not receive your recording or your voice profile.

HealthKit

Lull may request read-only access to sleep stages so it can fade the story out 5 minutes after you reach a non-awake stage. Sleep data is read on-device and used only for that purpose. We do not store, copy, or transmit the data.

Subscriptions and payments

All purchases go through Apple's StoreKit. We never see your payment information. Apple shares with us only an anonymous indication that you have an active entitlement.

Children's privacy

Lull is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices

  • You can edit or clear your name, city, and activity in Settings.
  • You can clear all chat history in Settings → Companion.
  • You can revoke HealthKit access in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Lull.
  • You can revoke microphone access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.
  • Uninstalling Lull deletes all of the above from your device.

Changes

If we ever materially change this policy we'll bump the date above and post a notice in-app for the next launch.
